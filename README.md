# Rod Ltd Sales Performance Analysis (KPI Dashboard)
Sales performance analysis for Rod Ltd using Excel to identify yearly revenue trends, top products, and growth opportunities.

## Project Overview
This project presents a sales performance analysis for **Rod Ltd**, a fictional company.  
The analysis evaluates key sales KPIs, regional performance, channel contribution, and product trends using Microsoft Excel.

*Rod Ltd is a fictional company used strictly for analytical practice.*

## Business Objective
To assess sales performance between 2014 and 2018, identify growth trends, performance gaps, and uncover actionable opportunities to improve revenue and market penetration.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning using Power Query
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- KPI Dashboard

## Dataset Description
The dataset consists of multiple tables including:
- Sales Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Regions and State Mapping

These were cleaned, modeled, and analyzed within Excel.

## Key Findings

### 1. Geographic Sales Concentration
Sales were heavily concentrated in **California, Illinois, Texas, and Florida**, spanning the West, Midwest, and South regions.

### 2. Channel Performance
The **Wholesale channel** consistently generated the highest share of total revenue and remains the dominant sales channel.

### 3. Northeast Under-Penetration
Despite its strong market potential, the **Northeast region (notably New York)** remains under-penetrated compared to other regions.

### 4. High-Performing Products in the Northeast
Products **7, 2, 6, and 4** showed strong SKU performance in the Northeast, indicating clear demand and expansion potential.

### 5. 2018 Growth Slowdown
Sales growth stalled in 2018 due to a **decline in units sold**, suggesting volume-related challenges rather than pricing issues.

### 6. Customer Loyalty Opportunity
Repeat customer behavior is evident, presenting an opportunity to introduce loyalty programs to increase purchase frequency and referrals.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase targeted sales and marketing efforts in the Northeast, especially New York
- Leverage high-performing SKUs to drive regional expansion
- Strengthen wholesale partnerships while exploring diversification
- Introduce customer loyalty and reward programs
- Investigate drivers behind declining unit sales to restore growth
